The Money Formulas (memorize!)

Price from margin: Price = Cost ÷ (1 − margin)
e.g. $9,000 cost, 25% margin → 9,000 ÷ 0.75 = $12,000
Markup → Margin: margin = markup ÷ (1 + markup)
e.g. 33.3% markup → .333 ÷ 1.333 = 25% margin
Accounting equation: Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity
Current ratio: current assets ÷ current liabilities (want > 1.0)
Working capital: current assets − current liabilities
Gross margin: (price − cost) ÷ price
